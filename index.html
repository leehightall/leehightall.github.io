About Me

李海涛2016年于曲阜师范大学获得“通信与信息系统”硕士学位，目前是东南大学生物医学工程在读博士，主要研究兴趣为机器学习和生物信息学。

Hai-tao Li received the master degree in Communication and Information System from Qufu Normal University in 2016. Now he is working toward the PhD degree in Southeast University. His main research interests focus on machine learning and bioinformatics.
